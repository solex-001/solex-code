About NACOSS

The National Association of Computer Science Students (NACOSS) is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing within the field of computer science. We aim to empower students with the skills and opportunities needed to excel in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

Join us for workshops, seminars, coding competitions, and networking events designed to enhance your academic and professional journey.
